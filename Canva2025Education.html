Canva Education Webinar Summary

An interactive overview of the new tools and resources from Canva designed to enrich the classroom experience.

Watch the Official Presentation

Your All-in-One Creative Canvas

Visual Suite 2.0 is a beacon of creativity, allowing you to craft comprehensive lesson plans and engaging student activities seamlessly within a single design. It transforms abstract concepts into colorful, interactive experiences.

With Visual Suite 2.0, lesson preparation becomes an inspiring journey, helping you captivate students' imaginations and foster a dynamic learning environment.

Bring Data to Life

Transform rigid, lifeless tables of data into vibrant, dynamic resources. Canva Sheets integrates features like Magic Charts and Magic Insights to illuminate trends and provide deeper understanding.

These tools empower you to manage student data with a finesse that turns analysis into an engaging educational narrative for personalized progress monitoring.

Your Creative Teaching Assistant

Canva's AI tools act as an artist's brush, helping you create tailored visuals and lesson plans with remarkable speed and ease. It's like having a skilled assistant ready to generate captivating graphics at the click of a button.

This technology streamlines the creative process, allowing you to focus on what truly matters: inspiring students and igniting their passion for learning.

Learning Through Play

The introduction of Canva Code opens a door to a world of interactive educational games, seamlessly blending technology with creativity. It's a powerful way for students to engage in learning through play.

Create custom games to tailor learning experiences to your curriculum, fostering an atmosphere of collaboration, critical thinking, and problem-solving.

Continuous Professional Growth

Canva's Design School is a treasure trove of free tutorials and resources. It offers a continuous learning journey, equipping you with the skills to refine and enhance your teaching practices.

Just as a gardener nurtures plants, Design School cultivates your professional development, ensuring you remain at the forefront of innovative teaching strategies.
