How to Write Effective Prompts

Use the "PARTS" framework for better AI responses

P
Persona
Identify your role to set context
A
Aim
State your objective with specific task
R
Recipients
Specify intended audience
T
Theme
Describe style, tone, parameters
S
Structure
Note desired output format

Prompt Template

I am a [Enter your persona]. [State your aim and recipients]. [Describe the theme and structure].
